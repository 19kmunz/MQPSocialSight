Design Overview

Facebook features an infinitely scrollable feed of photos, videos, and texts, both from people you know and suggested by Facebook. On Facebook you can build and maintain a profile to present yourself - your photos, thoughts, ideas and activities. You can connect with people you may know or groups of strangers by sharing or re-sharing posts, publicly commenting, or private messaging. Your Home feed includes suggested content, while the Feeds tab allows you to curate a filtered list of content you want to see, without suggestions.

Infinite Combined Feed

Your feed is infinite, meaning that there will never be a second page. The feed shows you posts by people you follow, as well as content recommended by an algorithm.

Curated Friends List

Maintain a list of people you want to connect with by sending and receiving friend requests, and adding your contacts. You can share posts, direct message, and post memories.

Post and Re-Share

Post text, image, or video content or repost content from other pages. You can post to all of your friends, specific people, or community-driven groups.
