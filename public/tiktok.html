Design Overview

TikTok is a video application that features videos of a maximum of 3 minutes, which you can watch or post. These videos vary vastly in subject, and a recommendation algorithm will attempt to show you videos you are interested in. If you view a video multiple times or like it, you are more likely to be recommended videos like it. You can follow creators and see them in your "Following" feed.
TikTok also features a live video functionality. You can go live or watch other creators, and buy TikTok coins to send to your favorites.

Infinitely Scrolling Feed

TikTok's home feed, called the "For You" page, utilizes a recommendation algorithm of videos. No need to follow topics or users, the algorithm will suggest other videos based on the ones you view and like.

Short Video Content

TikTok videos are a maximum of three minutes, with most running less than one minute. This makes it easy to watch videos like DIYs, recipes or makeup in a short period of time.

Record And Post

TikTok makes it easy to record your own videos with a handy set of tools, including timers and filters. You can use pre-existing sounds from a large library, or even duet someone else's video and post yours side-by-side.
