Design Overview

Every day, all users receive an alert that it is "Time to BeReal." The aim is to post a photo of yourself within two minutes of receiving an alert. Missing the timer has no consequences, other than your post being marked as late. After you post you can see your friends' posts for the day, comment on posts, and react using photos of your face. On default, your posts include your exact location. If this feature is desired, you can see the location of every BeReal in your memories. If not, you can turn it off!
BeReal is currently ad-free, meaning you will never see ads or sponsored posts. Instead you will see two feeds: "Friends," the people you have added, and "Discover," people who have their BeReal set to public.

Post Once A Day

Post a photo of yourself, using the front and back camera of your phone without filters, within two minutes of receiving a notification, to show what you are doing at a random time.

Simultaneous Posts By Friends

See what your friends are up to! Your friends receive a notification at the same time as you, so right after you post, you can see their posts.

View Memories

Revisit your old posts to see what you were up to in previous days, months and years!
