Design Comparison


Snapchat is primarily a messaging app, where you can send text, photo and video messages to your friends. You can send messages that disappear after opening, or post photos and videos as public stories that disappear after 24 hours. The app seeks to combine friends with entertainment, putting your friends' stories on the same page as creators and channels' stories.

Instagram began as a photo-sharing app, and has become a frankenstein of endless features: Post, like, comment, message, explore, shop, and more, it's all possible! You can still post photos (one at a time or multiple) as well as videos with captions to build a profile. Follow users and view their posts and stories in a home page, check out the Explore page to search and find new content to watch and follow.


Disappearing Messages

Send chats, photos and videos that disappear after viewing. Keep up friendships by maintaining a Streak when you and your friend both send at least one photo per day.

Fun Image Editing Tools

Snapchat's camera boasts many fun filters, from simple designs like smoothing skin and adding makeup, to full effects like animating your face or adding Augmented Reality (AR) objects.

Content and News

Snapchat displays Stories in a separate tab. This includes your friends' stories, but it also includes entertainment. These may be creators you recognize or news sources, but are often reposts and clickbait "shocking" and "satisfying" content.

Infinitely Scrolling Feed

Your feed is infinite, meaning that there will never be a second page. The Home feed shows you posts by people you follow with ads, and the Explore feed shows content recommended by an algorithm.

Photo/Video Based Posts

Post and view both photos and videos! These posts can disappear within 24 hours (as Stories), or stay on your profile, like a public photo album!

Share and Message

With direct messages, you can share photos and videos with your friends, and communicate privately! You can also enter "vanish mode," where messages disappear after view.
