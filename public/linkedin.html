Individual Professional Presentation Platform

Users: 310.6 million
Annual Revenue: $13.8 billion

Key Strengths
  1. Work-focused profiles for professionals
  2. Ease of applications for jobs
  3. Authentic personas
Key Warnings
  1. No financial support for creators
  2. Posts are mostly achievement based or for a “professional” persona
  3. Strongly advertiser based

Design Overview

LinkedIn features an infinitely scrollable feed of image, text and video posts by people you are connected to and posts they have liked. This feed can be sorted by default "Top" or a sorted "Recent" categories. You can find connections within your school or work organizations. All profiles will be either 1st degree (your connections), 2nd degree (connected to your connections) or 3rd+ degree connections (external).
LinkedIn also features a paginated feed of jobs, including jobs recommended by your profile information

Create a Professional Profile

LinkedIn encourages you to create a detailed profile of yourself to present to colleagues and employers. This includes a profile picture, education, work experience, and skills.

Apply To Jobs

You can search for open jobs by title, skill, company, or location. Once you find a job, you can view the description and apply or save the job to apply later.

Infinitely Scrolling Feed

Your feed is infinite, meaning that there will never be a second page. The feed combines the activity of people you follow with content recommended by an algorithm.
