Design Overview

Tumblr is a social media platform inspired by classic blogs. Due to this it enables more user control than other social medias, including fully-customizable blogs using HTML and extensive settings which include sorting the home feed by recommended or chronological, and turning off infinite scroll to add pages.

Combine Media and Styles

Combine text, image, video and audio into a single post. This allows for a lot of creative freedom in combining images and music, or posting detailed replies to posts.

Customizable Profile

Your profile is as customizable as you want! Stay simple by adding text and modifying your color scheme, or take your blog into your hands by adding custom HTML and CSS code.

Infinitely Scrolling Feed

Your feed is infinite, meaning that there will never be a second page. The feed shows you posts by people you follow, with minimal to no content recommended by an algorithm. This can be turned off on the web version.
