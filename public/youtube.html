Design Overview

YouTube is a free video-hosting website. It doubles as a social media and a search engine, where users can seek both entertainment and education. YouTube features videos of any length, with videos shorter than 60 second categorized as a "Short." Users can rate videos with a like or dislike, post and rate comments, and subscribe to keep up with their favorite creators' posts.

Algorithmic Recommendations

YouTube is known for its video recommendations, featured on the Home page. You will be recommended similar content to videos you watch. This is in part because YouTube relies on viewers to generate revenue through ad time.

Video Focused Posting

YouTube allows anyone to film and post videos or live streams. You must have a channel to post, which can be created with a Google account. YouTube also features community posts which may be text, images or polls, but these are often supplemental to videos.

Subscriptions Feed

Manage a list of subscriptions. These are channels you like and want to see more of. Instead of relying on recommendations, you will see these videos in a separate, chronological feed.
